About Me

Hi, I’m John Ansah, a detail-oriented and tech-savvy professional with experience in IT support, web development, and virtual assistance. I enjoy solving technical challenges, designing responsive websites, and helping people and businesses work smarter through technology. As a young person passionate about innovation, the GCGO of Job Creation strongly resonates with me. I’ve experienced how limited opportunities can affect young people, and this drives my commitment to building digital skills that create value and open doors, both for myself and others. I’m currently specializing in Data Analytics, where I turn raw data into meaningful insights that support better decision-making. This path aligns with my goal of becoming a Business Intelligence Manager and contributing to solutions that reduce youth unemployment. I focus on mastering strong fundamentals, building real-world projects, and communicating insights clearly. I learn fast, stay curious, and can easily get lost in “tech detective mode” whenever I’m exploring something new. With every project, I bring dedication, creativity, and a passion for using technology to make an impact. I enjoy combining technology, creativity, and organization to help individuals and businesses work smarter and achieve their goals. Whether it’s troubleshooting systems, building a digital presence, or streamlining daily tasks, I bring dedication and adaptability to every project I take on.
